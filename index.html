I fell into the role of their in house technican. I began to work on their brewers, espresso machines, and even plumbing. I was working crazy hours and had another Cafe reach out offering a better schedule. I moved to Houndstooth, where I managed all their espresso machines, brewers, and water filtration for locations in Austin and Dallas Texas. In 2016 I left Austin,TX to move to Seattle,WA. So I explored, played with turtorials online. I really enjoyed the trouble-shooting aspect and I was intrigued with tech companies that are making our lives easier.
